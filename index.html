    FAQ

    Questions answered. No runaround.

    How do I order online?

    Go to our menu page. Browse the dishes. Add what you want to your cart. Proceed to checkout and provide your delivery or pickup details. you'll get a confirmation right away.

    What are delivery times?

    Most orders arrive within 30 to 45 minutes. During peak hours it might take a bit longer. You'll see the estimated time before you confirm your order.

    Do you offer pickup?

    Yes. Choose pickup at checkout and we'll have your meal ready. You can pick it up at our location or use our drive-through window.

    Can I save items for later?

    Absolutely. Use the save for later feature on any dish. Your saved items stay in your account so you can order them whenever you want.

    What payment methods work?

    We take credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets, and cash on delivery. All payments are secure and processed instantly.

    Reach out to us anytime. We're here to help.
